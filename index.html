CIAO PARIS

Dear Friend,

I came to Paris on February 2nd

Excited and anxious for this adventure


I became friends in new ways

with new people,

Reconnected with old friends

in new ways too,

And nurtured my time alone.


I often feel out of time

(I’m almost always running late)

Let it be a blessing in disguise,

Because it’s only that I am excited to do so many things



Paris has been exhilarating,

Thank you for being part of it for me.


I will leave on the 22nd of December

Excited to go, sad to leave.


Let us convene for farewell drinks



Vendredi 19 Décembre, 19h

Point Éphémère, 200 Quai de Valmy, 75010 Paris

RSVP
