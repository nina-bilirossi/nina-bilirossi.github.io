CIAO PARIS

Dear Friend,

I often feel I am running out of time

(I almost always am running late)

Let it be a blessing in disguise

Because it’s only that I am excited to do so many things



Paris has been exhilarating



Whether our paths crossed for the first time,

Or if we already shared memories,

Thank you for being part of it for me.


I will now retrieve to Switzerland for hibernation (and more side quests).

Excited to go, sad to leave.


Let us convene for my hibernation launch party (farewell drinks).



Vendredi 19 Décembre, 19h

Point Éphémère, 200 Quai de Valmy, 75010 Paris



Nina
